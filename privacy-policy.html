Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how TubeSumTalk ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data.

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

By using the TubeSumTalk extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Required Permissions

TubeSumTalk requires certain permissions to function properly. Here's why each permission is necessary:

storage

This permission allows us to save your preferences (such as TTS voice, speed, pitch, summary type, and length) locally on your device. This ensures your settings persist between browser sessions.

activeTab

This permission allows the extension to interact with the currently active YouTube tab. It's necessary to extract video transcripts, inject the sidebar UI, and provide the summarization functionality.

host permissions (youtube.com)

This permission allows the extension to run on YouTube websites. It's required to detect when you're watching a YouTube video and to extract the video transcript.

Information We Collect

Information Collected and Stored Locally:

  • User Preferences: TTS voice selection, playback speed, pitch, summary type, and summary length.
  • UI State: Sidebar width, collapsed state, and other UI preferences.

Information Processed Temporarily:

  • Video Information: Video ID, title, and transcript of the YouTube video you're watching.

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Your browsing history
  • Your YouTube watch history
  • Your Google account information
  • Any information from non-YouTube websites

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving the TubeSumTalk extension's functionality:

  • Video transcripts are processed to generate summaries.
  • User preferences are stored to personalize your experience.
  • UI state is saved to maintain your preferred layout.

We do not:

  • Sell or rent your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Track your browsing activity across websites
  • Store your data on our servers (except temporarily for processing)

Data Storage & Security

TubeSumTalk stores your preferences locally on your device using the browser's storage API. This data never leaves your device except when:

  • Video transcripts are sent to our backend server for processing by the Gemini AI model.
  • The backend server temporarily processes this data to generate summaries and then discards it.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your data. All communication between the extension and our backend server is encrypted using HTTPS.

Third-Party Services

TubeSumTalk uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: Used to generate summaries from video transcripts. When you use our extension, video transcripts are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. Please refer to Google's AI Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Web Speech API: Used for text-to-speech functionality. This is a browser API and does not send data to external servers.

User Rights

As a user of TubeSumTalk, you have the right to:

  • Access: View the data stored locally by the extension through your browser's developer tools.
  • Modify: Change your preferences through the extension's settings.
  • Delete: Clear all stored data by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser storage.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
